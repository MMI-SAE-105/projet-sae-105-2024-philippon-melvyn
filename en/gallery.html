*Poster of the movie Backdraft*

Synopsis

This dramatic thriller follows two firefighter brothers from Chicago as they confront a series of mysterious arson cases. The film delves into their complex relationship and highlights the dangers and sacrifices of being a firefighter.

Director: Ron Howard

Release date: 1991

*Poster of the movie Reign of Fire*

Synopsis

In a desolate post-apocalyptic world ravaged by fire-breathing dragons, a small group of determined survivors must fight relentlessly for their survival. They strive to eradicate the deadly creatures that now dominate the planet.

Director: Rob Bowman

Release date: 2002

*Poster of the movie Only the Brave*

Synopsis

This film, inspired by a true story, follows an elite group of firefighters as they fight to contain a massive forest fire in Arizona. It highlights the courage and sacrifices of these fire heroes.

Director: Joseph Kosinski

Release date: 2017
