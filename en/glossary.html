Glossary

Lighter

A lighter is a portable device used to produce an instant flame, utilizing a reservoir of flammable liquid or gas and a mechanism to create a spark.

Archaeologist

An archaeologist is a scientist who studies the material remains of ancient civilizations to understand their history and culture.

Narration

Narration is the art of telling a story by using a narrative to convey events, emotions, and experiences.

Book

A book is a collection of pages bound together, containing text, images, or illustrations, meant to be read for information, entertainment, or education.

Form

A quick registration form is a document designed to rapidly collect essential user information for signing up for a service or event.
